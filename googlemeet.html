How to Create a Google Meet and Invite People

Step 1: Go to Google Meet

Navigate to Google Meet using your web browser.

Step 2: Create a New Meeting

Click on "New Meeting" and choose one of the following options:

  • Start an instant meeting - Begin a meeting immediately.
  • Schedule in Google Calendar - Set up a future meeting in Google Calendar.

Step 3: Set Up Meeting Details

If you choose to schedule in Google Calendar, fill in the meeting details like date, time, and guests, then click "Save".

Step 4: Invite Participants

After starting or scheduling the meeting, invite people by:

  • Adding email addresses - Click "Add others" and enter email addresses.
  • Sharing the meeting link - Copy the meeting link and share it via email, chat, or other communication platforms.
