How to Put a Virtual Background on Google Meet: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Open Google Meet

1. **Go to Google Meet**: Open your web browser and go to Google Meet.

2. **Sign In**: Ensure you're signed in with your Google account.

Step 2: Start or Join a Meeting

1. **Create a Meeting**: Click on "New meeting" and select "Start an instant meeting" or "Schedule in Google Calendar".

2. **Join a Meeting**: If you have a meeting link, click on "Join with a code" and enter the meeting code provided.

Step 3: Access Background Settings

1. **Before Joining**: On the preview screen before joining the meeting, locate the "Change background" icon. It looks like a person with a plus symbol (+) in the background.

- Alternatively, click on the three dots (More options) at the bottom right corner of the video preview and select "Change background".

2. **During the Meeting**: If you're already in the meeting, click on the three dots (More options) at the bottom right corner of the screen and select "Change background".

Step 4: Choose a Background

1. **Blur Background**: You have the option to slightly blur your background or apply a stronger blur. These options are usually the first two icons.

2. **Pre-Loaded Backgrounds**: Scroll through the pre-loaded virtual backgrounds provided by Google Meet and select one by clicking on it.

3. **Upload a Custom Background**: To use your own image, click on the "+" button to upload an image from your computer. Select the image you want to use and it will be applied as your virtual background.

Step 5: Adjust and Apply

1. **Preview**: Preview your selected background in the video preview window.

2. **Apply**: Once satisfied with your selection, simply close the background settings window, and your virtual background will be applied automatically.

Tips for Best Results:

  • Lighting**: Ensure you have good lighting to help the virtual background work effectively.
  • Background**: Use a plain, uncluttered background to avoid interference with the virtualbackground effect.
  • Camera Quality**: A higher quality camera can improve the virtual background effect.

By following these steps, you can easily set up a virtual background on Google Meet, making your meetings more private and visually appealing.
