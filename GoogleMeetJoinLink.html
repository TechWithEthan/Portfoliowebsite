How to Join the Google Meet using a link

Here are a few steps to join a Google Meet using a meeting link

  1. Join a video meeting with a meeting link.
  2. Open the chat message or email with the meeting link and click the meeting link.
  3. Click Ask to join.
  4. When someone in the meeting gives you access, you'll join it.
  5. Wait for the meeting organizer to let you in.
  6. Once you're in, you can start participating in the meeting.
  7. For more information about Google Meet, you can visit the Google Meet website.
