Automatically Admit Participants in Google Meet

Chrome

  1. Open Chrome browser.
  2. Visit Google Meet Auto Admit for Chrome (link not provided for security reasons - search online).
  3. Click "Add to Chrome".
  4. In the popup, click "Add Extension".
  5. Open meet.google.com or click here to start a meeting.

Firefox

  1. Open Firefox browser.
  2. Visit Google Meet Auto Admit for Firefox (link not provided for security reasons - search online).
  3. Click "Add to Firefox".
  4. In the popup, click "Add Extension".
  5. Open a new tab and type meet.new and hit enter to start a meeting.
  6. Click the checkbox to activate the auto-admit addon.

Edge

  1. Open Edge browser.
  2. Visit Google Meet Auto Admit for Edge (link not provided for security reasons - search online).
  3. Click "Get".
  4. In the popup, click "Add Extension".
  5. Open meet.google.com or click here to start a meeting.
