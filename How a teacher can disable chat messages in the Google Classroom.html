Teacher's Guide - Google Classroom

How a Teacher Can Disable Chat Messages in Google Classroom
  1. Open Google Classroom and sign in with your Google account.
  2. Click on the class for which you want to disable chat messages.
  3. In the top right corner, click on the gear icon to open class settings.
  4. Scroll down to the "General" section and find the "Stream" settings.
  5. Select "Only teachers can post or comment" to disable student posts in the stream.
  6. Save your settings.

Additional steps to disable chat in Google Meet:

  1. Open Google Meet and start or join a meeting.
  2. Click on the three vertical dots in the bottom right corner, select "Settings" and go to the "Host controls" tab.
  3. Toggle off the "Chat" option to disable chat messages for participants.
